About the BeanCat Works

Hi!! My name is David Capuano. I live in Brunswick, Maine, USA. I have worked in the IT field for over 30 years and I would like to share some of that knowledge and experience with others.

My experience started in the aerospace industry in the 1970′s. I moved to working in the banking and brokerage field in New York City in the 1980′s, and worked for Merrill Lynch for nearly 18 years. After that, I worked in the Pharmaceutical industry for major names such as Bristol-Meyers.

I have also taught in two major universities: Rutgers University, and New Jersey Institute of Technology. These were great experiences and I really loved sharing my knowledge.

I have a great blog - just go to the "blog" link at the top of the page. If you are interested in the software I developed, check it out. It costs nothing to try in the Beta stage. Please contact me for a login and password.

Thanks, Dave Capuano
